Space Shooter — Privacy Policy

A. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we handle your private data.

B. Storing and processing data

Data is processed and stored only on your device. It is not sent over Internet to be processed in other places. The developers of the app do not have any knowledge of the data stored by the application.

How we collect data

The app can collect the following types of data:

  1. Touch input
  2. Inputted text
  3. Any other kind of data that is required for the app to work

D. Information security

We take reasonable technical precautions to prevent the loss, alteration, and misuse of your data.
